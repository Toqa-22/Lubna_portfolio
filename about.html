About

Between the brush and the classroom

An Art Educator and Visual Artist with a passion for combining creativity with education — and a growing practice shaped by cultural exchange.

Me

A journey that started with a pencil

I am an Art Educator and Visual Artist with a strong passion for combining creativity with education. My journey in art started from a deep love for drawing and painting, which later expanded into teaching and leading interactive workshops for students of different ages.

Teaching

I believe that art education plays an important role in developing creativity, confidence, and emotional expression. My teaching approach focuses on hands-on learning, exploration, and encouraging students to express their own ideas freely.

My Interest

Alongside art education, I am also interested in cultural exchange, especially through language learning and Chinese culture, which I integrate into my creative practice.
